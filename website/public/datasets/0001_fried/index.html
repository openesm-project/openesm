Dataset Information#

  • First Author: Fried
  • Year: 2021
  • DOI: https://doi.org/10.1177/21677026211017839
  • Topics: mental health, social contact, COVID
  • Participants: 80
  • Time Points: 56
  • Beeps per Day: 4
  • Sampling Scheme: 4x/day
  • Link to Data: https://osf.io/mvdpe/
  • Link to Code: https://osf.io/mvdpe/
