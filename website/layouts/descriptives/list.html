Item-level distributional statistics computed from within-person summaries across openESM datasets. Select a dataset and item to explore the distribution of participant-level statistics.

Methods note

These statistics are computed automatically using standardized exclusion criteria (participants with fewer than 5 observations or zero variance are excluded) and are intended for orientation only. The exclusion thresholds and statistical choices (e.g. skewness estimator, bandwidth for mode detection) may not match the preprocessing choices appropriate for your research question. Do not use these figures directly in scientific publications without verifying the underlying data and replicating the analyses yourself.
